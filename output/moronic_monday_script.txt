Deep Dive: The Tax Trap
"I'm married filing jointly. We both work and checked the boxes, but we still owe $10,000 this year."
Reality: The 2(c) checkbox on the W-4 form is notoriously glitchy.
Advice: Use the IRS Tax Withholding Estimator every quarter.

Deep Dive: Solo 401(k) Time Machine
"Is it too late to contribute to my retirement for the 2025 tax year?"
Reality: You can contribute to a Solo 401(k) or SEP IRA for 2025 until April 15, 2026.

Deep Dive: Mortgage Points
"My lender is asking if I want to 'buy down' my rate. Is that a scam?"

Deep Dive: Is Ethereum Dead?
